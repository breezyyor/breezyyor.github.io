About

I'm Breezy Yor -- a video and content creator who works at the intersection of storytelling, sound, and speed. My process usually starts with a moment worth reacting to, and ends with it living natively across Instagram, X, Facebook, and YouTube -- each version cut for how that platform actually gets watched.

I'm just as comfortable directing a scene through a text prompt in an AI video tool as I am color-grading a traditional edit. That mix lets me move fast without cutting corners on craft -- whether the brief is a brand campaign, a music-led visual, or a cultural moment that needs to be seen.

Recent work has leaned into creative activism -- building full campaigns (song, poster, video, caption) around issues that matter to a younger audience, and shipping them at the speed the internet actually moves.
